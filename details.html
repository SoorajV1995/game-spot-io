Gaming Life

A gamer is a person who plays interactive games, either video games, board games, skill-based card games or physical games, and plays for usually long periods of time. (In some countries, such as the UK, the term "gaming" can also refer to legalized gambling, which can take both traditional tabletop and digital forms.) There are many different gamer communities around the world. Since the advent of the internet, many communities take the form of Internet forums or YouTube/Twitch virtual communities, as well as in-person social clubs.
